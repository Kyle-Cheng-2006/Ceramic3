About The Creator


Hello! I am Kyle Cheng, the creator of these ceramic guides! Feel free to click on the navigator links to explore the websites. You can also subscribe to me on YouTube (short films and video essays) and Instagram (ceramics artwork progress). I am also a multimedia artist. I have worked with ceramics, glassblowing, 3D mixed media, printmaking, photography, and painting. My favorite medium to work in is ceramics and photography. I am currently interested in conceptual and installation artwork, shifting from the 2D to the 3D plane. Acoustic, video, and kinetic work is the direction my work is currently moving. I wish to achieve a master's degree in fine arts to become an Arts Professor.
