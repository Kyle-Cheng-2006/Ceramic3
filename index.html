About The Creator


Dear Reader, my name is Kyle Cheng. I am the creator of these ceramic guides. Feel free to click the navigator buttons to explore the different websites. Most images and any links or buttons you see on the websites are clickable and open avenues to discover more about the art of ceramics. You can also check out my YouTube and Instagram accounts, where I post my films and artwork, linked in the navigator section. I am a multimedia artist with experience in ceramics, glassblowing, 3D mixed media, printmaking, photography, and painting. I am currently interested in conceptual and installation artwork. In the future, I wish to achieve a master's degree in fine arts to become an art professor.
